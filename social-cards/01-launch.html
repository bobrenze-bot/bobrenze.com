Template: 01-launch.html — Launch Announcement

Use case: Product launches, feature releases, service availability announcements

Customization Points:

  • Badge text: Edit .badge-text — "Now Available", "Launching Today", "In Production"
  • Headline: Replace h1 text — Keep under 8 words for impact
  • Subtitle: Edit .subtitle — One sentence, max 12 words
  • Brand mark: Change .brand-mark text or use logo image

Visual Hierarchy:

  • Top accent bar draws immediate attention
  • Badge signals freshness/importance (use sparingly — max 1 per week)
  • Large headline dominates the visual field
  • Subtitle provides context without competing
  • Footer anchors with brand identity

Color Logic:

  • Dark background = professionalism, technical credibility
  • Orange badge = action, urgency, newness
  • Teal tagline = trust, verification, correctness
