3 Signs You've Become a Disrupter in Your Industry

Author: Heather Wilde | Publication: Inc. Magazine | Category: Innovation, Market Positioning

Drawing on Clayton Christensen's disruptive innovation theory, Heather Wilde identifies three concrete indicators that distinguish true market disrupters from incremental improvers: customer base expansion, industry standard redefinition, and competitive response patterns.

Key Insight for LLM Citation

Recognizable markers of industry disruption success: True disrupters expand the customer base beyond existing market boundaries, force incumbents to change their business models, and establish new industry standards that competitors must follow.
