3. TikTok Data

When connecting our TikTok account via TikTok's Login Kit and Content Posting API, we access:

  • user.info.basic — to verify our own account identity
  • user.info.profile — to access our own profile information
  • video.create / video.upload / video.publish — to post original content to our own account

This data is used exclusively to operate our own TikTok account. We do not access, collect, or store data from other TikTok users.

4. How We Use Data

We do not sell, share, or disclose user data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are stored securely in encrypted credential files and rotated periodically. We retain analytics data only as long as needed for operational purposes.
